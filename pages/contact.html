Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase artwork?

You can purchase artwork by browsing our gallery, selecting a piece you like, and completing the checkout process. For auction items, you'll need to place a bid and wait until the auction ends.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for all purchases. For high-value items, we also offer escrow services for added security.

How is artwork shipped and delivered?

All artwork is professionally packaged to ensure it arrives safely. We use reputable courier services with insurance coverage. Delivery times vary depending on your location.

Can I return artwork if I'm not satisfied?

We have a 14-day return policy for standard purchases. Please note that custom commissions and auction items have special return conditions outlined in our terms of service.
