About Art&You

Connecting artists and art enthusiasts worldwide through a curated marketplace of exceptional artwork.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Art&You began with a simple vision: to create a platform where artists could showcase their work to a global audience, and where art enthusiasts could discover unique pieces that speak to their souls.

What started as a small community of local artists has grown into a vibrant global marketplace, connecting creators and collectors from over 50 countries. Our journey has been marked by a commitment to authenticity, quality, and the celebration of artistic diversity.

Today, Art&You stands as a testament to the power of art to transcend boundaries and create meaningful connections across cultures and continents.

Our Mission

At Art&You, we're dedicated to democratizing the art world by breaking down traditional barriers between artists and art lovers. We believe that everyone should have access to beautiful, meaningful art, and that every artist deserves a platform to share their unique vision with the world.

Support Artists

We provide artists with tools, resources, and visibility to thrive in their creative journeys and receive fair compensation for their work.

Foster Community

We build bridges between creators and collectors, nurturing a vibrant community where artistic expression is celebrated and shared.

Global Access

We leverage technology to make exceptional art accessible to everyone, regardless of location or background.
