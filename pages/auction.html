How Auctions Work

Participating in our auctions is easy and secure. Follow these simple steps to start bidding on your favorite artworks.

2. Place Your Bid

Browse active auctions and place bids on artworks that catch your eye. You'll be notified if you're outbid.

3. Win & Collect

If your bid wins, you'll be notified immediately. Complete payment securely and receive your new artwork!
